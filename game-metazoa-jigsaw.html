Metazoa Jigsaw

Puzzles
Metazoa Jigsaw is a different flavor of classical jigsaw game. Arrange the jigsaw pieces of a picture to form the original picture. There are 2 modes in the games. Each mode has 24 levels. You must complete a level within time limits.
