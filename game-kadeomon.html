Kadeomon

Arcade
Kadeomon is a 2D cute little platformer where you have to collect all of the guavas(fruits) while avoiding the enemies, pins, spikes and reach the Exit door in order to go to the next level. There are 8 levels to play and the difficulty increases as you proceed.
