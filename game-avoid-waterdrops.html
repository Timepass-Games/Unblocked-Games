Avoid Waterdrops

Puzzles
Avoid Waterdrops is an arcade kids game where you need to control flipped umbrella to avoid water drops. But the clouds are in the sky, and it seems that will be rain. Avoid water drops and earn points. Move on the left or on the right side. As the game is in progress the rain will be harder and faster. You can be hit three times for drops before the game is over.
