Match 3

Puzzles
Match 3 is a puzzle thinking game with dozens of challenging levels where your ingenuity and creativity are put to the test. Study carefully the image that is given to you, and choose the 3 figures that best simplify it. What is the first thing that comes to mind? What objects could you use in that situation? Show your skill creating visual associations and prove how smart you are!
