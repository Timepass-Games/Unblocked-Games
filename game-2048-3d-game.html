2048 3d Game

Girls
2048 3D is a fun running game with little balls of numbers. Its not difficult to control your ball, swipe to control your ball, and collide with balls with the same number to merge into a higher-numbered ball. Watch out for obstacles on the road, they will make your ball smaller! The bigger the number you merge, the higher the score you get. Enjoy!
