Monkey Mart

Shooting
In Monkey Mart, a game developed by TinyDobbins, you take on the role of a cute monkey who has just opened their own supermarket. Your task is to manage the store by planting fruits, stocking stands with bananas, corn, and other products, and managing the cashier desk. Clients will come to shop at your store and you need to be attentive to make sure they are satisfied. As you progress, you'll have the opportunity to upgrade your character, unlock new work stations and recruit additional workers to help you with store management. It's an idle management game that offer a fun and engaging gameplay for all ages.
