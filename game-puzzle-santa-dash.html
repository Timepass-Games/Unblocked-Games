Puzzle Santa Dash

Puzzles
Puzzle Santa Dash us a puzzle bubble shooter game. This time you will control Santa and he will shoot with Christmas bubbles. Shoot on the bubble that have same color as shooting bubble. Match three or more same colored bubbles to destroy them. Shoot all bubbles before they reach the bottom of the game. Move the Santa to the position that you want to shoot and tap on screen to shoot the Christmas bubble.
