Pumpkin Slide Reps

Puzzles
A Halloween pumpkin slicing game with a twist. You only get one slice to draw out but the twist is that the slice keeps on repeating. You'll need to keep this in mind if if you want to slice all the pumpkins without hitting a skull. See if you can complete all 24 levels. If you get stuck trying to complete a level you can always click the Video button on the main menu to see a walkthrough of all of the levels.
