Batman Match 3 Puzzle

Puzzles
Another brain training game for you is Batman Match 3 Puzzle. This game will help you to have a different view of the thinking game. You can play this game with your friend and try it at the ben10aliens.net website. Batman is having a very troublesome problem. Our Batman character has mixed up several colored marbles and is trying to sort them into distinct categories. He will be punished if he does not return the number of marbles to the original. However, he alone cannot arrange all those marbles and hopes you can help him. Your task in this game is to match marbles of the same color. And of course, it will require time, help Batman, so he doesn't get punished.
