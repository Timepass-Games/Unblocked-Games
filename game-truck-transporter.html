Truck Transporter

Racing
In the game Truck Transporter you will drive a truck of the company KamAZ. Your task is to deliver a certain cargo to the final point at each level. But first you have to download it with a special tap. Capture objects and send them to the back. Try to keep them as tight as possible so that they do not fall out during the trip. And the road will be quite difficult. If you carry at least eighty percent of your cargo, your trip in Truck Transporter will be counted.
