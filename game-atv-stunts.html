ATV Stunts

Racing
ATV Stunts is the second sequel to the 3D car racing simulation. The player has 4 different colored vehicles to switch between. One large map contains lots of obstacles and places to do tricks. The game is suitable for all ages. Let's ride a atv on a crazy map. Choose from four vehicle colors. Do you prefer green, blue, red or yellow-red formula. Discover all the places where the real action starts. Bridges, ramps, tunnels, way in the air is just a fraction of what you will find on the map. Don't hesitate and get on your super fast vehicle! Become an atv champion. Have fun.
