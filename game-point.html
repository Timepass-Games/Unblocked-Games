Point

Puzzles
Point is a puzzle shooter game. There will be a balls in the roll and only one red ball in it. You need to hit the red ball with the ball at the bottom of the screen that rotates left and right .Shoot at the right moment when you think the ball is in the direction of the red ball you need to hit. When you hit the ball, the line of balls will appear elsewhere and you have to do the same, hit again. If you miss twice in a row the game will be over for you.
