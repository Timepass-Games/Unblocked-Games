Money Honey

Arcade
As a pure collecting dress-up running arcade game, Money Honey allows you to help a beauty find rich men and pay for her favorite clothes including bags, shoes, skirts, and pants. All men have their values and will provide her with different amounts of money. If you want her to change rich suits, you need to help her find richer jobs!
