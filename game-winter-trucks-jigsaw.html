Winter Trucks Jigsaw

Puzzles
This truck game is a jigsaw puzzle game. The game has twelve images with Winter trucks. You can play in all of them. But first you need to go to he first one and to solve, then you can unlock next image. So tap on the available image to play and choose the mode that you want to play. You can play easy with 25 piece of the image, medium with 49 or hard with 100 pieces. Tap on the pieces to move and put on the position to get the image.
