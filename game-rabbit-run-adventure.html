Rabbit Run Adventure

Puzzles
This is arcade adventure game with cute rabbit. Your job is to help the rabbit to pass all obstacles on the platforms and to get save to the finish line. Try to pass all levels and to win at this game. You need to jump over shapes, animal creatures and blades, or to go down under some obstacles. You will have three life's at each level. If you lose a life you will have to go back to the beginning of the game.
