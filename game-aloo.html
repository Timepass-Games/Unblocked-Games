Aloo

Arcade
Aloo is a 2D platformer where you play as the main character called Aloo, the potato man, and your mission is to collect energy drink bottles while avoiding the saws, giant blades, spikes and ball monsters. There are 8 levels to play and the difficulty increases as you proceed.
