mySolar Build your Planets

Shooting
Play as a God, build your own solar. Roaming the universe to collect GP (God point) and MP (Mass point) to grow your solar. Defeat all emery solars, absorb their resources. It is special type of solar system games, you need to build your own solar system by collecting asteroid to get MP, and destroy enemy planet to get GP. Using GP you can create orbits, and add slot on the orbit. Using MP you can create planet or sun. Also each planet can also able to add satellites. When enter each level, it is simulate a sandbox universe. You can enter the level unlimited of times, to get MP and GP again and again.
